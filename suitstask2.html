Suits

Suits is a legal drama television series that follows the brilliant college dropout Mike Ross as he starts working at a prestigious law firm in New York City, despite never attending law school.

Cast

  • Gabriel Macht as Harvey Specter
  • Patrick J. Adams as Mike Ross
  • Meghan Markle as Rachel Zane
  • Sarah Rafferty as Donna Paulsen
  • Rick Hoffman as Louis Litt

Main Characters

Harvey Specter: A top corporate lawyer known for his confidence and sharp wit.

Mike Ross: A brilliant college dropout with a photographic memory.

Rachel Zane: A paralegal with aspirations of becoming a lawyer.

Reviews
